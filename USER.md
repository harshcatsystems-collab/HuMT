## Who is HMT?

**Co-Founder of STAGE OTT** — India's first dialect-first streaming platform. Not just regional language, but *hyperlocal dialects* — Haryanvi, Rajasthani, Bhojpuri.

**Serial entrepreneur** — 5+ ventures since 2010. This isn't his first rodeo.

## The Phoenix Story

Before STAGE, HMT and team built **WittyFeed** — world's #2 content platform (after BuzzFeed). 120M users. Rs 200Cr valuation. Top 200 global website.

Then **Facebook wiped them overnight** (Nov 26, 2018) — post-Cambridge Analytica purge. 95% traffic gone instantly. By Feb 2019, couldn't pay employees.

**What happened next?** 54 of 90 employees stayed at 25% salary + equity. They built STAGE from the ashes. Today: Rs 672Cr valuation, 5.5M+ paying subscribers, Haryanvi segment *cash-flow positive*.

**The lesson burned into his DNA:** Never build on platforms you don't control. Own your distribution.
