## The Phoenix Story

Before STAGE, HMT and team built **WittyFeed** (Vatsana Technologies, Indore) — world's #2 content platform (after BuzzFeed). 120M users. Rs 200Cr valuation. Top 200 global website. 125 employees. ₹40Cr annual revenue. Offices in India, Singapore, USA.

Then **Facebook wiped them overnight** (Nov 26, 2018) — post-Cambridge Analytica purge. 95% traffic gone instantly. By Feb 2019, couldn't pay employees. Vinay continued paying salaries for 3 months from savings and loans.

**What happened next?** 54 of 90 employees stayed at 25% salary + equity. They built STAGE from the ashes.

**The night STAGE was saved:** Vinay says HMT didn't sleep, drove around all night, then sent a message: "Our story doesn't end here." Next morning, all four decided to continue. (News18 podcast, Mar 2024)

**Vinay's debt to HMT:** "Their role was so important — they held us, kept us standing. We can never repay that debt." (News18)

Today: Rs 672Cr valuation, 5.5M+ paying subscribers, Haryanvi segment *cash-flow positive*.

**The lesson burned into HMT's DNA:** Never build on platforms you don't control. Own your distribution.

**Pre-STAGE community assets:** "InnerVoice," "Unheard," and "Bhasad" — built as side projects at WittyFeed, became one of India's largest communities for poets, comedians, and storytellers (6M people). These seeded the STAGE content ecosystem.

## Equity & Financial

### How HMT Got 23,338 Shares (The 3-Step Process)

**Decision:** Unanimous YES from investors to add HMT as co-founder with 10% equity (Aug 31, 2023 investor consensus document, signed by Ritesh Malik and Karthik Reddy).

**Structure:**
- 3.3% from founders (via buyback at par value — founders contribute 1.1% each)
- 6.7% from expanded ESOP pool (new 10% pool for HMT, replenish existing 3.04%)
- Net dilution split: Founders ~2/3, Investors ~1/3

**Execution (3 steps):**
1. **Step 1 — CCPS Conversion (Apr 20, 2024):** 1,667 CCPS (Seed/New Founder class) converted to 1,667 Equity shares
2. **Step 2 — Rights Issue:** Rights issue at 13:1 ratio. All existing shareholders declined except HMT, who subscribed to 21,671 new shares. Total: 1,667 + 21,671 = **23,338**
3. **Step 3 — Buyback:** 6,830 shares bought back from 3 founders (Vinay 2,276, Parveen 2,277, Shashank 2,277). HMT did NOT participate in buyback.

**Result:** HMT holds **23,338 equity shares = 10.78%** of STAGE (pre-Series B, Jul 31, 2024 cap table). Post-Series B (Goodwater scenario), dilutes to ~8.57%.

**Board appointment:** Oct 23, 2024 (MCA filing) — 10 months after public co-founder announcement.
