## The Truth About Baby Costs

Babies don't need much. The industry wants you to think otherwise.

**What babies actually need:**
- Safe place to sleep
- Food (breast milk is free, formula isn't)
- Diapers
- Basic clothing
- Car seat
- Love (unlimited, free)

**What the industry sells:**
- $500 strollers, wipe warmers, designer onesies they'll wear twice

Be intentional. Buy less. Buy smart.

## 💡 Money-Saving Strategies

### Before Baby
1. **Ask for hand-me-downs** — Babies use things for months, not years
2. **Buy second-hand** — Cribs, strollers, carriers (check safety standards)
3. **Wait on non-essentials** — You'll figure out what you actually need
4. **Create a registry** — Let others gift the expensive stuff
5. **Stock up during sales** — Diapers, wipes don't expire fast

### After Baby
1. **Consider cloth diapers** — High upfront, big savings over time
2. **Breastfeed if possible** — Formula is expensive
3. **Buy in bulk** — Diapers, wipes, formula
4. **Shop off-season** — Winter clothes in summer
5. **Resell what you don't need** — FB groups, OLX
