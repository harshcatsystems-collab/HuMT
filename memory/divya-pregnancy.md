### Anomaly Scan - What They Check
- Head circumference (HC)
- Abdominal circumference (AC)
- Femur (thigh bone) length
- Humerus (arm bone) length
- Brain, face, spine, heart, stomach, bowel, kidneys, limbs
- Amniotic fluid level
- Placenta position

### OGTT (Glucose Test) - How It Works
1. Fast overnight (8-12 hours)
2. First blood sample (fasting)
3. Drink 75gm glucose in 200ml water
4. Blood at 1 hour and 2 hours

**Normal ranges:**
- Fasting: <92 mg/dL
- 1 hour: <180 mg/dL
- 2 hours: <153 mg/dL

## Symptom Management

### Heartburn
- 6-inch thick pillow
- 2 tsp Apple Cider Vinegar in warm water (empty stomach)
- Sleep 3 hours after dinner
- Banana
- Malasana pose
- Warm water with ushir (vetiver root)

### Nausea
- Ginger tea / ginger candy
- Lemon water / smell lemon
- Mint tea
- Jeera water (cumin)
- Elaichi (cardamom) chewing
- Keep dry snacks (biscuits) by bed
- Light snack before getting out of bed

### Headaches
- Regular sleep schedule
- Hydration (2.5-3 liters/day)
- Small frequent meals (avoid low blood sugar)
- Good posture
- Cold compress on forehead
- Warm compress on neck/shoulders
- Rest in dark, quiet room
- Avoid: stress, bright lights, loud noise, strong smells

### Sleep Issues
- Left side sleeping
- Pillow between knees
- Small pillow under abdomen
- Head & ear massage with medicated oil
- Toe/sole pressing before bed
- Yoga Nidra

### Growing Belly Discomfort
- Don't stand long
- Sit straight
- Firm mattress
- Side sleeping with pillow between legs

## Foot Massage Guide

**Position:** Sit comfortably, legs slightly elevated

**Technique:**
1. Gentle, slow strokes
2. Start from toes → heel → ankle
3. Light circular motions on sole
4. Gentle squeezing of toes and arch

**Duration:** 10-15 minutes per foot

**Oils:** Coconut, Almond, or Sesame oil
