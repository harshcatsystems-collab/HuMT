## Content Decisions

### Strategic Decisions
1. **Weekly Episodic Publishing:** Vinay Singhal endorsed weekly episodic drops over binge for web series (Bhairavi, JJJ). "We don't have any benefit from a binge watch." Vaibhav initially proposed this for JJJ.

2. **Title Testing Impact:** Kunal Kumrawat's experiment proved **title has direct correlation with Thumbnail CTR**, also impacts TCR, SR, D1 retention. Example: "Meri Biwi Ka Raaz" (55.3% CR) vs "Mrs. Kinnar" (50.7%) vs "Kiya Love Paaya Dhoka" (45.8%).

3. **Retention-First Approach:** Multiple titles following retention-first strategy (Chalava BH dub, Jay Santoshi Maa, Pyaar ke Parwane, etc.). Performance + retention assets created simultaneously.

4. **Microdramas as Premium Feature:** Leadership decided microdramas are premium-only (trial active/subscribed users only). Pranay Merchant communicated this during development.

5. **Content Strategy Forum:** Started March 2025 by Parveen Singhal. Extensive discussions on:
   - ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) development across 3 markets
   - Persona mapping (15-45 demographic as core, 20% spillover)
   - "Gut → Data → Gut" approach (Netflix reference)
   - Content selection framework being developed
   - Fishbone analysis on retention problems
   - No Rules Rules book discussions for org culture

6. **CTA Testing:** All ads running with 2 CTAs: "₹1 Me 7 Din" & "Watch Now" — comparing conversion paths.

7. **Celebrity Integration:** Parveen raised discussion on optimizing celebrity costs for STAGE. Randeep Hooda partnership proved effective (D0 SR 28%, 50% better).

8. **Narrative Locking SOP:** Being created for quarterly title narratives + promo shoots with celebs/influencers.

9. **Default App Language:** Parveen pushing to set English as default. Decision pending.

10. **Web Series Repackaging:** anish proposed repackaging existing web series (Palli Ka Pilla, Mafia Diaries, Police and Crime) into feature films and relaunching.

## Patterns & Observations

- **Rajasthani:** Most structured pipeline. Priyanka Gupta runs a tight ship — regular release plans, tracking numbers, city tours, CAC solvers. Highest volume of organized micro dramas.
- **Bhojpuri:** Strong creative team led by anish (CAC solver MOMs every week). Heavy promo shoot culture. Sneha Singh manages release threads meticulously. Growing original production.
- **Haryanvi:** Longest-running market. Rajesh is central figure for releases. vaibhav drives strategy. More established but less structured tracking than RJ.

### Recurring Themes
1. **Title renaming is epidemic:** Almost every micro drama and many films change names between production and release. Anushka had to issue formal request to mention old names in brackets.
2. **Asset delivery delays:** Constant chase for performance assets (promos, statics, trailers, seconders). sonu kumar's templated requests appear repeatedly.
3. **QC bottlenecks:** Content QC, tech QC, and compliance steps frequently delay releases. Multiple instances of content stuck.
4. **Dubbing as growth lever:** Cross-culture dubbing is a major strategy. HR→RJ, RJ→BH, BH→HR, and all→GJ for Gujarati launch.
5. **CAC Solver meetings:** Regular cross-functional meetings to optimize creative performance. Both BH (anish) and RJ (Vaibhav Goyal) running weekly.
6. **Creator ecosystem development:** Film festivals (RIFF, Haryana Film Festival), partnerships (Ghazipur Lit Fest, UP T20 League, Haryana Carnival), celebrity integrations.
7. **AI adoption emerging:** AI posters being used (criticized for quality — Vipan Malawat). AI song created via suno.ai. Simora.ai UGC tested. AI lip-sync explored.
8. **Micro dramas growing fast:** Started mid-2025, now significant volume across all cultures. Mandatory trailer requirement instituted.
9. **Social media as acquisition channel:** Reels going viral drive app installs. Kayantar reel (1.4M views), Chakka Jaam song reels (10K+), MV2 city tour reels (1M).
10. **Compliance/sensitivity awareness:** Multiple reminders about caste-related language, religious content accuracy, meme appropriateness.
