## Normal Ranges (Pregnancy)

| Category | Systolic | Diastolic |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Normal | <120 | <80 |
| Elevated | 120-129 | <80 |
| Stage 1 Hypertension | 130-139 | 80-89 |
| Stage 2 Hypertension | ≥140 | ≥90 |

**Pulse:** Normal resting is 60-100 bpm. Pregnancy increases baseline by 10-20 bpm, so up to ~110-115 can be normal in 2nd trimester.
