## Key Insight
Current product is **in-match only** — inherently ephemeral. Users have no reason to open STAGE before/after matches.

**Pre-match, post-match, between-match content creates habitual engagement.**

---

## The Gap
Star Sports has regional dialect commentary behind a paywall. STAGE differentiation: **free + authentically regional**, not unique language coverage.

The property gap: "regional language exists" → "regional language feels authentic"

### 1. Commentary
**Model:** Hybrid
- **Human:** Haryanvi (highest engagement — 532 WC listeners, 11.7 avg min)

**Decision pending:** (due tomorrow EOD)

### 1. Distribution

### 2. Ops at IPL Cadence

**Challenge:** 60 matches in 51 days. Sometimes back-to-back. World Cup ops model doesn't scale.

**Target State:** Minimal manual intervention. Automate everything automatable.

**One Human Touchpoint Retained:** Producer's brief — editorial layer that makes commentary feel match-specific. Streamlined to 10 minutes, not eliminated.
