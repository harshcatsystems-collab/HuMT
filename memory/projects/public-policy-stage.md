## 2. Risk Navigation (Most Critical)

### Why It Matters
> "Companies are rarely popped overnight" — Vinay Singhal

But when regulatory risk hits, you need:
- An experienced policy person tracking regulatory shifts
- A contact who can engage government officials in high-stakes moments
- Someone monitoring legislative consultation processes

### Examples of Risk
- RMG (Real Money Gaming) regulatory crackdown
- NPCI/auto-pay issues affecting subscriptions
- Content regulation shifts

### Required Capabilities
- Monitor regulatory changes proactively
- Gather information on upcoming legislation
- Participate in consultation processes
- Build relationships before crisis hits
