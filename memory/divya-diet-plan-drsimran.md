## 🎯 Core Principles

### 1. Iron + Calcium = Never Together
**This is CRITICAL.** Iron and calcium bind together and form a different molecule — iron won't absorb.

| Situation | What to Do |
|-----------|------------|
| Dinner has paneer/dairy | Take iron supplement at **bedtime**, not dinner |
| Morning calcium (milk) | Gap of **2 hours** before any iron-rich food |
| Taking iron supplement | Wait **2 hours** before milk |

**Why this matters:** Dr. Simran's client had very low hemoglobin despite taking iron + calcium consistently — turns out she was taking them 30 min apart. Iron wasn't absorbing. She ended up needing an IV drip.

### 2. Dinner Target: 7:30 PM
- Fasting sugar is spiking — early dinner helps manage this
- Sleep 3 hours after dinner (bedtime ~11 PM)
- Milk can come at bedtime, so you won't feel hungry

### 3. Small Meals, Often
- Eat every 2-3 hours
- Never skip meals — blood sugar drops
- Keep dry snacks (biscuits) ready

## ⏰ Daily Timing Guide

### 🌅 Morning

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Wake up | Fresh air, morning sunlight (10-15 min) |
| Breakfast | Light, nourishing meal |
| **30 min after breakfast** | Tea (if having) — NOT with breakfast |

**Tea replacement:** Turmeric + nutmeg milk (boiled, cooled to lukewarm)
- Caffeine is addictive — expect headaches for ~1 week when quitting
- After 7-8 days, withdrawal stops

### ☀️ Daytime

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Post meals | Walk after 10-15 min rest |
| Throughout | Jeera water — make 1 liter in morning, sip throughout |
| Hydration | 2.5-3 liters/day minimum |

**Non-veg Timing:**
- ❌ **NOT at dinner** — takes too long to digest, triggers acid reflux
- ✅ **5-6 PM snack** — this is the best time for chicken/non-veg
- Never post 6 PM

### 🌙 Evening & Night

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 5-6 PM | Non-veg (if having), heavier snack |
| **7:30 PM** | Dinner (target!) |
| Post dinner | Light walk if possible |
| 10 min after iron supplement | Amla juice (diluted, small amount) |
| 2 hours after Amla | Milk (bedtime) |
| ~11 PM | Sleep (left side, pillow between legs) |

## 🥛 Milk & Turmeric Protocol

**When hungry or need calcium:**
1. Boil milk with a pinch of **turmeric + nutmeg powder**
2. Let it cool to lukewarm (not too hot)
3. Drink

**Turmeric Quality Matters:**
- Best: **Kacchi haldi** (fresh turmeric) ground at home
- Good: Organic/gastromeric powder
- Avoid: Regular market haldi — has colors, no real curcumin benefit

---

## 🍋 Amla Juice Protocol

**Product:** Kapiva Naisamla Juice (concentrate)

**Timing:**
1. Take **10 minutes AFTER iron supplement** (Vitamin C helps absorption)
2. Don't take together — can trigger nausea suddenly
3. Small amount, diluted
4. Wait **2 hours** before milk

**Why:** Vitamin C + iron = better absorption. But need the gap to avoid nausea.

---

## 💧 Jeera Water Protocol

1. In the morning, boil 1 liter water with jeera (cumin)
2. Let it cool
3. Sip throughout the day, especially after meals
4. Aids digestion

## 🍗 Non-Veg Rules

| ❌ Don't | ✅ Do |
|----------|-------|
| Chicken at dinner | Chicken at 5-6 PM snack |
| Non-veg post 6 PM | Weekend lunch is okay |
| Night non-veg (acid reflux) | Early enough to digest before dinner |

**Reason:** Non-veg takes much longer to digest. If eaten at dinner (7-7:30 PM), it won't digest before sleep → acid reflux issues.

## 🥥 Coconut Water Rules

- ❌ **Not daily** (despite being "healthy")
- ✅ **2-3 times per week MAX**
- Has natural sugars — not ideal when managing sugar levels

## 🤕 Nausea & Headache Management

### For Nausea:
- Clove remedy: Roast ajwain + 10-15 cloves, make potli, smell it
- Ginger tea / ginger candy
- Lemon water or just smell a lemon
- Jeera water
- Chew elaichi (cardamom)
- Dry biscuits before getting up

### For Headaches (tea withdrawal):
- Stay hydrated (this is #1 cause!)
- Caffeine withdrawal lasts ~7-8 days
- Replace with turmeric milk
- Cold compress on forehead
- Foot massage with sesame oil

### Homemade ORS (when feeling very low):
- Lemon water + salt + little sugar
- Only use when feeling extremely low
- Packet ORS only if extreme
