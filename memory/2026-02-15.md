# 2026-02-15 — Sunday

## Morning Brief Delivery Failure & Fix
- Morning brief cron (`daily:morning-brief`) failed at 9 AM IST with "cron delivery target is missing"
- Same error hit `capability-verify` and `security-audit`
- Root cause: `to` field (HMT's Telegram ID) was missing from delivery config — `channel: "telegram"` alone wasn't enough
- Fixed all 4 delivery-enabled jobs with explicit `to: "8346846191"`
- `healthcheck:update-status` was also missing `to` — caught on double-check
- Morning brief ran successfully after fix, delivered to HMT
- HMT asked if it was because last engagement was on WhatsApp — no, it was the missing `to` field

## Lesson
- **Always include both `channel` AND `to` in cron delivery config** — channel alone can fail with "delivery target is missing"
- This is the second time delivery config broke (first: Feb 13 missing channel, now: missing `to`). Pin BOTH fields always.
