## Critical Findings

1. **The D0→D1 cliff is 50pp** — 65% watch on D0, 15% on D1. This is THE growth bottleneck. Everything downstream (AHA, habit, mandate) depends on getting users past D1.

2. **34% of trial users never watch anything.** Another 45% watch exactly 1 day. Only 20% watch 2+ days. Only 5% reach the engagement threshold for habit formation.

3. **D0 watchtime predicts everything.** 0-min watchers: 0.6% AHA. 120+ min watchers: 13.4% AHA. 20x difference. But 51% of users watch <5 min on D0.

4. **Even heavy D0 watchers (60+ min) have a 60-70% D1 dropout rate.** The D1 problem isn't just about D0 engagement — there's a structural return barrier.

5. **Futwork OB calling shows ZERO incremental conversions** (Feb 20-23). The operation processes 5-7K leads/day but may not be adding value over organic.

6. **Web trials growing fast (17% → 37%)** but cancellation rates are similar to app. Web isn't solving the quality problem, just shifting channel mix.

7. **Autopay (D-1) is the hero** — captures ~50% of all renewals. Manual renewal is tiny. Mandate infrastructure is working.

8. **AHA rate gap: 12.5% all-time vs 3.9% recent 30-day.** The recent cohorts are much worse at reaching AHA. Acquisition quality degradation or AHA threshold too high?
