## 🌾 GRAINS & MILLETS

| Item | Why | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Brown rice | Healthier carb, fiber | Replace white rice |
| Jowar (sorghum) flour | Roti, flakes, idli | High fiber, gluten-free |
| Bajra (pearl millet) flour | Khichdi, roti | Good for digestion |
| Foxtail millet | Vermicelli (sevaiyya) | Light, easy to digest |
| Ragi/Nachni flour | **MUST ADD** 3-4x/week | Regulates appetite, helps lactation |
| Rolled oats | Masala oats breakfast | Not instant — plain rolled |
| Quinoa | Dinner option | Complete protein |
| Whole wheat/Khapli wheat | Roti, pasta | Better than refined |
| Dalia (broken wheat) | Dinner porridge | High fiber |

| Item | Why |

| Store-bought yogurt | Make curd at home |
| Biscuits, cookies, rusk | Bakery = refined carbs |
| Sodas, colas | Sugar, carbonation |
