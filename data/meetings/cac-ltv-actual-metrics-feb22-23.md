### The Full Damage Calculation

**If Randeep budget was reallocated to Feature Films:**

| Scenario | CAC | TCR | Trials Generated | TC Generated |
|----------|-----|-----|-----------------|--------------|
| **Actual (Randeep)** | ₹247 | 26.5% | 1,519 | 402 |
| **If Feature Films** | ₹304 | 38.1% | 1,233 | 470 |
| **Delta** | +₹57 CAC | +11.6% TCR | -286 trials | **+68 TC** |

**The math:**
- ₹3.75L at ₹247 CAC = 1,519 trials (actual)
- ₹3.75L at ₹304 CAC = 1,233 trials (if features)
- **Volume loss:** 286 fewer trials (-18.8%)
- **Quality gain:** 470 TC vs 402 TC = +68 converted trials (+16.9%)

**Net outcome of Randeep shutdown:**
- Lose 18.8% trial volume
- Gain 16.9% converted trial volume
- **Win if:** Subscription rate + renewal are equal across cohorts
- **Lose if:** Randeep cohort had higher downstream conversion (unlikely given poor TCR)

**Projected monthly savings:**
- 2-day spend: ₹3.7L
- Monthly: ~₹55L
- Matches Acquisition Pod claim: "₹20L/week" = ₹80L/month (same order of magnitude)
