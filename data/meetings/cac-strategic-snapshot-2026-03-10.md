**Not ₹270 as claimed** — but this reveals something important.

### 2. Rajasthani is Your Most Efficient Market
**Not Haryanvi** (the mature one you'd expect).

| Market | CAC | TCR | Cost per Converted Trial |
|--------|-----|-----|-------------------------|
| **Rajasthani** | ₹263 | 41.0% | **₹641** ⭐ |

**Feb 22-23: ₹3.7L on Randeep (8.4% of total) = ₹55L/month burn on broken acquisition.**

Shutdown was correct. Question is: how long was it running before you caught it?
