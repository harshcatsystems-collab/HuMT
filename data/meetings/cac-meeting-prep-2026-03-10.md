## Format-Wise Benchmarking (Critical Gap)

### The Problem (from Acquisition Pod)

**Current state:** "95% budget on features → blind to other format performance"

**Old model:** One-size-fits-all (₹250 CAC for everything)  
**New model:** Segmented benchmarks

| Dimension | Segmentation Needed |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **Format-wise** | Microdrama ≠ Feature ≠ Long Series |
| **Channel-wise** | Meta App ≠ Google Web ≠ Meta Web |
| **Market-wise** | Haryanvi (mature) ≠ Gujarati (new) |

### Format Performance Benchmarks (from LTV Optimization)

**Watch frequency multipliers:**

| Format | Frequency vs Baseline |
|--------|---------------------|
| Feature film | 1.5x |
| Binge series | 6x |
| Long series | 16x |
| Microdrama | 8-10x |

**TCR implications:**
- Feature films: "Dead content" for TCR, can confuse users on platform value
- Binge series: Higher TCR, builds habit moments

**Acquisition driver hierarchy:**
1. Feature films (reach, but poor activation)
2. "Contank videos" (viral hooks)
3. Binge series (best for trial quality)

**Strategic insight:** "Solder content" (viral, shareable) more important than "real content" for initial acquisition — but retention requires the opposite

## Meta/Google Optimization: Trial Retention Postback

### Proposed Strategy (from Acquisition Pod, Mar 3)

**Setup:** Binary value signal across full 7-day trial window

| Event | Postback |
|-------|----------|
| Trial cancel (D0-D6) | Revenue = 0 |
| Mandate realized (D7) | Full subscription value |

**Benefits:**
- Feeds Meta's Value-Based Optimization
- Feeds Google's Smart Bidding
- Platforms learn to shift spend toward users who don't cancel

**Why full trial window (not just D0):**
- TCR can happen any day before D7
- 70% of cancellations happen by D5
- Continuous signal = better platform learning

**CAC impact:** Platforms optimize for trial quality → fewer wasted impressions → lower CAC over time (2-4 week learning curve)

**Current acquisition flow:**
1. Acquire user at ₹270 CAC
2. User subscribes but doesn't watch
3. Likely churns at first renewal
4. **Effective LTV:** ~₹800 (one payment) instead of ₹1,106 modeled
5. **Actual LTV/CAC:** ~3x instead of 3.69x

**Real CAC ceiling if 60% of subs are dormant:**
- Blended LTV = 0.4 × ₹1,106 + 0.6 × ₹800 = ₹922
- For 1:1 CAC:LTV in 3mo → max CAC = ₹307 (not ₹150)
- **We're at ₹270 — closer than the ₹150 target suggests**

**Implication:** Fixing dormant activation is AS IMPORTANT as reducing CAC

## Risk Flags

### 1. The ₹150 Target May Be Unrealistic

**Context:** Historical best (excluding outlier) = ₹217 (Feb '25) with minimal volume (69k subs)

**Risk:** Forcing CAC to ₹150 might:
- Collapse acquisition volume below viable scale
- Require quality trade-offs (worse TCR/SR from lower-intent traffic)
- Take 6+ months of optimization, not 2 weeks

**Mitigation:** Phase the target — ₹200 by April, ₹175 by June, ₹150 by Sept

---

### 2. Market Mix Shift Could Increase Blended CAC

**Context:** New markets (Gujarati, Bhojpuri) need higher CAC for land-grab

**Risk:** Even if per-market CAC improves, blended could worsen if new market volume grows faster than mature market volume

**Example:**
- Jan '26: 80% Haryanvi @ ₹200, 20% Gujarati @ ₹300 → Blended ₹220
- Mar '26: 60% Haryanvi @ ₹180, 40% Gujarati @ ₹280 → Blended ₹220 (no improvement despite both dropping)

**Mitigation:** Track per-market CAC separately, accept blended CAC rise if market mix is strategic

---

### 3. TCR Benchmark Mismatch

**Context:** Current TCR 45-50% vs "32% benchmark"

**Question:** Where did 32% come from? If it's a legacy benchmark pre-format segmentation, it may not apply

**Risk:** Chasing wrong benchmark wastes effort

**Mitigation:** Validate benchmark source, update to format-specific benchmarks

---

### 4. Dormant Crisis Undermines All CAC Optimization

**Context:** 1.1M paying users not watching = broken activation, not broken acquisition

**Risk:** Hit ₹150 CAC but users still don't watch → LTV stays low → economics still broken

**Mitigation:** Parallel workstream on activation (not just acquisition optimization)
