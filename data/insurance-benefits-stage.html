STAGE Employee Insurance Benefits

STAGE Employee Insurance Benefits — Complete Guide

Prepared from Insurance Orientation Session, March 18, 2026

Overview

STAGE provides four insurance policies for employees: 1. GHI — Group Health Insurance 2. GPA — Group Personal Accident Insurance 3. GTL — Group Term Life Insurance 4. Flexi Topup — Optional self-paid coverage upgrade

Plus wellness benefits via the Even App and HealthyShore platform.

1. GHI — Group Health Insurance

What’s Covered

  • Standard hospitalization expenses
  • Medical treatments within India
  • In-patient care

What’s NOT Covered (Exclusions)

Category Exclusions
Treatments Abortion, sleep disorders, nasal sinus surgeries
Equipment Cochlear implants
Services Tele-consultation (covered separately via Even app), air ambulance
Other Foreign treatments (outside India), organ donor expenses

Waiting Periods

  • Initial waiting period: 30 days from policy start
  • Pre-existing diseases: 2 years

2. GPA — Group Personal Accident Insurance

Coverage Details

Benefit Amount
Sum Insured ₹10,00,000 (₹10 lakh)
Covers Employee only
Accidental Death 100% of sum insured (₹10 lakh)
Permanent Total Disability 100% of sum insured (₹10 lakh)
Accidental Hospitalization Up to ₹1,00,000 (₹1 lakh)
Child Education Benefit ₹5,000 per child (max 2 children)
Ambulance Expenses ₹5,000 per accident

What’s Covered

  • Road accidents
  • Air accidents
  • Train accidents
  • Accidents on-duty or off-duty
  • Terrorism-related injuries
  • Worldwide coverage — accidents outside India are covered

What’s NOT Covered

  • Intentional self-injury
  • Suicide or suicide attempts
  • Drug/substance abuse
  • Unlawful activities (e.g., driving without a valid license)
  • Adventurous/extreme sports
  • Acts of war
  • Medical hospitalization for illness (non-accident related)

5. Even App — Teleconsultation Services

Coverage

  • Unlimited teleconsultations
  • Covers employee AND all dependents
  • No payment required per consultation

Available Specialists (25+)

  • General Physicians
  • Dermatologists
  • Pediatricians
  • Nutritionists / Dieticians
  • Clinical Psychologists
  • Physiotherapists
  • And more…

Consultation Duration

Specialist Type Duration
Standard consultation 15 minutes
Nutritionist / Dietician 30 minutes
Clinical Psychologist (mental health) 45 minutes

How to Use

  1. Download the Even app
  2. Open app → tap “Consult an Even Doctor”
  3. Select a doctor (you can choose previously consulted doctors for continuity)
  4. View doctor’s profile — experience, languages spoken
  5. Book by selecting date and time slot
  6. Enter reason for consultation / symptoms
  7. Upload any relevant documents (blood reports, test results) — optional but helpful
  8. Receive email + app notification confirming booking
  9. Get reminder 5 minutes before consultation
  10. Video call with doctor
  11. Receive prescription + consultation note via email (includes diagnosis, management plan, prescribed medicines, further investigation recommendations)

6. Flexi Topup Policy (Optional Self-Paid Upgrade)

What It Does

Allows you to increase your sum insured beyond the base GHI coverage.

Example: Base GHI = ₹10 lakh + Flexi Topup = ₹30 lakh → Total = ₹40 lakh

Key Features

Feature Details
Policy Type Retail (in your name, not group policy)
Premium Based on your age + dependents’ ages
Health Checkup Required No
Declaration Required Good health declaration (15 illnesses listed)
Policy Issuance Emailed within minutes of payment
Room Category Up to single private AC room

How the Deductible Works

You must first exhaust your base policy (or pay out of pocket) before Flexi Topup kicks in.

Example: - Hospital bill: ₹12,00,000 - Base GHI coverage: ₹10,00,000 - First ₹10 lakh paid by base GHI - Remaining ₹2 lakh paid by Flexi Topup - Remaining Topup balance: ₹28 lakh (if you bought ₹30 lakh topup)

Waiting Periods

Type Duration Exception
Initial waiting 30 days Waived if hospitalized due to accident (covered from Day 1)
Pre-existing diseases 2 years No exception

What’s NOT Covered

  • Maternity — not covered under Flexi Topup
  • Pre-existing diseases (within 2-year waiting period)

When to Purchase

  • Can buy anytime before you need treatment
  • If diagnosed with any condition before purchase, it counts as pre-existing (2-year wait applies)

How to Purchase

  1. Open HealthyShore app
  2. Navigate to upgrade/topup section
  3. Fill good health declaration
  4. Make payment
  5. Policy copy emailed within minutes
