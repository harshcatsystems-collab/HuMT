4. Watcher% + Habit% as North Star Metrics

Why these metrics matter more than daily watch conversion:

  • Watcher% = Leading indicator of product-market fit. If users don't watch once, they'll never renew.
  • Habit% = Leading indicator of retention. 8+ watch days in M0 predicts 60-80% mandate survival.
  • Daily watch conversion = Lagging indicator. Measures today's outcome, not tomorrow's renewal.

The 10x Habit Gap

Only 3.07% of M0 users form a habit. Among watchers (34%), that's 9% habit formation rate. This means:

  • 91% of users who watch don't come back 8+ times
  • The product hooks users on first watch, but doesn't build a routine
  • Likely issue: Not enough new content to sustain weekly watching
Hypothesis: Users exhaust their dialect's library in 2-3 watch sessions. Without fresh releases, they churn. Content velocity (new releases/week) is the bottleneck, not acquisition.
