LVU Graveyard: The Case for Writing Off Carrier Ghosts

The Numbers

375K never-watched LVUs
0.26% daily conversion (~975/day)
-20% pool shrinking (expiring, not engaging)
33% of entire dormant pool

The Position

These are overwhelmingly carrier billing ghosts. They didn't choose STAGE. They don't know they're subscribed. Keeping them in the count:

  • Inflates subscriber metrics
  • Dilutes all engagement KPIs
  • Wastes CLM resources
  • Creates false sense of scale
Recommendation: Create a "Qualified Subscriber" metric excluding never-watched LVUs >90 days dormant. Report both numbers to the board. Operate on qualified subscribers internally.
