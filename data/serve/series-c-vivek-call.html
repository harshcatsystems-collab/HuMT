Vivek's Key Strategic Advice

On positioning:

"If you just pitch it as an AI business, people will ask why the impact hasn't shown up in the P&L."

Suggested alternative: "This is the business, this is the P&L. We pivoted to AI-native 3-4 months ago. Here's the new product lines and what AI does to economics."

On the pitch approach:

  • Vinay prefers pitching without slides (momentum speaker, uses DocSend)
  • Vivek: "There's so many parts to this story. Use slides but don't follow them slide-by-slide."
  • Optimize slide flow for reader; story flow for presenter

On deck vs memo:

  • Vinay asked if a 5-6 page memo would be better (used this approach pre-Series A)
  • Vivek: "I come from Amazon culture, we banned PowerPoint. But in VC world, people are trained to consume decks."
  • Verdict: Use a deck. Memo works when you're doing ₹100Cr+ revenue and 150% YoY growth — "then you can do whatever you want"

On urgency:

"Everything you've built in AI can be replicated by somebody else. The only advantage is you have to move fast. That's why you're raising now. That's why you want to go to 14 markets."
