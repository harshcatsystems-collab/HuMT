# M0 App Open Analysis — Diagnostic Findings

**Date:** March 3, 2026  
**Analyst:** HuMT  
**Context:** Vismit + Kawaljeet correction to V4 "acquisition quality collapse" conclusion

---

## Executive Summary

**The engagement drop is driven by APP OPEN DROP, not app open→watch funnel degradation.**

Trial engagement data (T6 users = 3+ watch days during trial) proves acquisition brought engaged users. The problem is **post-subscription**: users aren't opening the app as frequently.

---

## Data Analysis: Dec 2025 vs Feb 2026 M0 Cohorts

### App Open % by Trial Engagement Tier

| Tier | Trial Behavior | Dec 2025 | Feb 2026 | Change |
|------|---------------|----------|----------|--------|
| **T6+** | 3+ watch days (trial-engaged) | 38.0% | 37.1% | **-0.8pp (-2.2%)** ↓ |
| T5-T7 | 5-7 watch days | 24.3% | 23.7% | -0.6pp (-2.3%) ↓ |
| T2-T4 | 2-4 watch days | 12.8% | 12.5% | -0.3pp (-2.0%) ↓ |
| T1 | 1 watch day | 6.3% | 5.9% | -0.3pp (-5.1%) ↓ |
| **T0** | 0 watch days (never engaged) | 1.8% | 1.3% | **-0.4pp (-25.4%)** ↓ |
| **Overall** | All M0 users | 16.6% | 16.1% | **-0.5pp (-2.9%)** ↓ |

---

## Key Findings

### 1. ✅ App Open Drop Confirmed

**All tiers declined**, regardless of trial engagement:
- Trial-engaged users (T6+): -2.2% decline
- Never-engaged users (T0): -25.4% decline
- Overall: -2.9% decline

This is **not** a funnel problem (where app opens stay stable but watch conversion drops). Users simply aren't opening the app as frequently.

### 2. ✅ Vismit + Kawaljeet Were Right

The existence of **T6+ tier with 38% app opens** proves:
- Acquisition brought users who engaged during trial (3+ watch days)
- These are NOT "low quality" users
- The problem is **post-subscription engagement**, not acquisition

**V4's "acquisition quality collapse" conclusion was wrong.**

### 3. ❓ Root Cause: Organic vs Reengagement Drop

The data shows **app opens dropped**, but **not why**:

**Hypothesis A: Organic Drop**
- Content library didn't match post-trial expectations
- Interest faded naturally
- Product-market fit issue for subscription phase

**Hypothesis B: Reengagement Drop**
- Push notification volume/CTR declined
- Email campaigns reduced or stopped
- Retargeting ads paused or reduced

**To isolate:** Need reengagement campaign data (Dec vs Feb):
- Push notification sends & CTR
- Email campaign volume & open rates
- Retargeting ad spend & impressions
