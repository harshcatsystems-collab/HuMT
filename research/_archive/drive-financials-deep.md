### Key Finding on 23,338 vs 23,060

Two parallel calculations existed:
- **V1 (PC-workings-HMT):** Rights issue ratio 12.83:1 → 21,393 new shares → 1,667 + 21,393 = **23,060**
- **V2 (PC-workings-HMT_V2 / ShineWing):** Rights issue ratio 13:1 → 21,671 new shares → 1,667 + 21,671 = **23,338**

The **V2 calculation (23,338)** was adopted as final. The native Google Sheet (Jun 20) still shows 23,060 (V1) — it was never updated. The July 31 cap table and all subsequent documents use **23,338**.

### How HMT Got 23,338 Shares (The 3-Step Process)

1. **Step 1 — CCPS Conversion:** 1,667 CCPS (Seed/New Founder class) converted to 1,667 Equity shares on Apr 20, 2024
2. **Step 2 — Rights Issue:** Rights issue at 13:1 ratio. All existing shareholders declined except HMT, who subscribed to 21,671 new shares. Total: 1,667 + 21,671 = **23,338**
3. **Step 3 — Buyback:** 6,830 shares bought back from 3 founders (Vinay 2,276, Parveen 2,277, Shashank 2,277). HMT did NOT participate in buyback.
