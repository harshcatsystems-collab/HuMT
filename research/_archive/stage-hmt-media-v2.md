## Google Cloud Case Study — STAGE

**Source:** cloud.google.com/customers/stage

STAGE is a Google Cloud customer case study. Key quotes from Shashank Vaishnav:
> "There are about 200 dialects spoken across India's population of about 1.4 billion people... while multinational and India-based services concentrate on speakers of English and the dominant dialects in India, there are very few places speakers of dialects and local dialects can go to access quality content."

> "We identified 50 dialects across the country with a minimum potential audience each of 50 million people."

Tech stack: Google Cloud (Compute Engine, Cloud Storage, Cloud CDN, Cloud Load Balancing), Flutter for iOS/web apps, planned AutoML for recommendation engine.

VM provisioning: 15 seconds (vs 90 seconds on previous provider).

Written ~mid-2021 based on metrics (2M installs, planning Series A, 20K+ paying subscribers).

**No HMT mention** in this article.

**URL:** https://cloud.google.com/customers/stage
