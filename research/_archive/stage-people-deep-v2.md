### Career Arc
1. **2010:** Started "Badlega Bharat" — online youth awareness org at SRM
2. **2010:** Started Facebook page "Amazing Things in the World" → 1M followers in 6 months
3. **2012-13:** Built stupidstation.com → substantial AdSense revenue
4. **2014:** Co-founded WittyFeed with Parveen & Shashank → grew to world's #2 viral content platform. 120M users. Rs 200Cr valuation. Rs 25.9Cr revenue FY16.
5. **Nov 26, 2018:** Facebook wiped WittyFeed overnight (Cambridge Analytica aftermath). 95% traffic gone.
6. **2019:** Built STAGE from ashes. 54 of 90 employees stayed at 25% salary + equity.
7. **Present:** CEO of Rs 672Cr valued company, 5.5M+ paying subscribers

### Management Style & Philosophy (from interviews/podcasts)
- **"Confidence stack of India"** — "Once people start feeling comfortable with who they are, they feel confident, and that's how societies thrive. Like there's an India Stack, we are the confidence stack of this country." (Rising Bharat Summit 2025)
- **Anti-colonial baggage:** "We suppress our own culture and carry an inferiority complex around it."
- **Building entire film industries:** "Haryana today has 150 production houses which work with us regularly. We produce 50 original movies in each of these three markets every year."
- **Confrontational about Bharat's paying capacity:** "People who think Bharat doesn't pay for digital content have a limited understanding. It's a lazy statement made from an AC office."
- **Vision:** "Five years from now, every single regional culture of India and every Indian who comes from them would be proud of their cultural identity."
- **Future ambition:** "Tomorrow, if it takes us to making Disneyland for each one, then we will make that as well."

### Technical Journey (from Masai School Code Talk, Oct 2023)

**Key revelations from the talk:**
- Started by reading about Facebook, Google, Infosys and deciding "I'm going to do something around the internet"
- Studied top 60-70 social networking sites; conceived "smart buyers" concept (audience-decided pricing)
- **WittyFeed scaling story:** Started on shared hosting → site crashed when posted to "Amazing Things in the World" page (10K instant users) → learned about AWS on Google in 2-3 hours → launched 32GB RAM server → crashed again → learned load balancing. "I kept learning things throughout the process."
- **Learning philosophy:** "If you think you'll sit and learn something and then implement it, you won't learn. You learn by doing."
- **On timing:** "The best time to build a startup is when you're in your college."
- **STAGE origin from his perspective:** After Facebook killed WittyFeed, "We were literally crying." Took loans to pay salaries. Called town hall — offered half salary + equity. **55 people stayed.** "Now we have to grow for these people." 4-5 months brainstorming sessions. Identified gap in northern dialects. Name "Stage" chosen because "most people know the meaning irrespective of which language they speak" + aspirational value.
