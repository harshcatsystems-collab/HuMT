### Scope — 5 Modules:

**Module 1: Vision, Mission & Values Articulation (3 weeks, ₹8L)**
- Leadership workshops, employee surveys
- SWOT vetting, design principles
- Forbes-style workshops for mission statement drafting
- CEO/Board validation

**Module 2: Level & Title Architecture (4-5 weeks, ₹10L)**
- Job evaluation for 80-100 roles against Aon's 5 compensable factors
- Unified level structure across departments
- Level descriptors
- Recommended title options (e.g., Associate → VP hierarchy)

**Module 3: Technical Competency Framework (4-6 weeks, ₹10L)**
- Competency library leveraging Aon's industry-validated dictionary
- SME surveys for role-competency-proficiency mapping
- P1 (Learner) through P4 (Expert) proficiency definitions
- Example: Software Engineering job family detailed

**Module 4: Behavioral Competency Framework (4 weeks, ₹3L)**
- Leadership 1:1s + Focus Group Discussions
- Define Growth Vectors:
  1. Look Beyond The Obvious (Foresight, Big picture, Detail orientation)
  2. Outcomes Matter More Than The Craft (Outcome orientation, Solution mindset)
  3. It's Only Impossible Until It's Done (Ambition, Experimenting, Resilience)
  4. Responsibility Is Taken Not Given (Learning orientation, Ownership, Empowerment)
  5. Success Is A Team Sport (Collaboration, Collective Accountability)

**Module 5: Compensation Benchmarking (3 weeks, ₹5L)**
- Peer basket finalization
- Mapping to Aon job families
- Compa-ratio, market positioning, catch-up analysis

### Commercials:
- **Total:** ₹36L → **Discounted to ₹26.5L** (26% discount)
- **Timeline:** 8-11 weeks total
- **Payment:** 50% on signing, 50% on report delivery

### Key Insight:

**Participants:** Prerna Prerna, Tarang Doshi, Nikil, Manasvi Dobhal

### Core Product Initiative — Reels/Shorts:
**Problem:** Significant portion of users open the app but don't watch any content.

**Solution:** Introduce **reels format** to drive consumption starts:
- NOT an endless feed — includes pauses prompting users to start watching full titles
- Leverages existing marketing content (influencer promos) within the app
- Multiple entry points: homepage section, content detail page, deep links
- Requires CMS for reel content management

**Strategic Decisions:**
- Immediate priority: Use reels to drive consumption starts for existing long-form content
- Reels-specific original shows: longer-term strategic decision
- Potential future: Reels as free entry point → trial → subscription
- Distant future: Ad model on reels

### Homepage Redesign Debate:
**Three options presented** — major disagreement on navigation:
- **"Coming Soon" section:** Prerna strongly advocated keeping it in navbar (retention driver, "notify me" clicks prove value). Tarang wanted to remove it (limited content, lacks trailers/context, homepage real estate too valuable).
- **Watchlist:** Currently broken at product level
- **Downloads:** Considered valuable for trial users
- **Search:** Tarang wanted rightmost position; Prerna argued second position is more reachable
- **Dialect change:** Surprisingly high interaction rate — implies content is language-filtered

### Key Insights:
- ~4 new titles per month for "coming soon" → not enough for constantly fresh section
- Cross-dialect users are edge cases but dialect switch has high click rate
- Content team needs to produce trailers/teasers — currently lacking
- Sprint was starting that day; goal to finalize details by following Friday
