## Product Details

### Original Vision (pyoopil.com — Wayback Machine, Jan 2016 & Nov 2016)

**Tagline:** "Communities around courses"  
**Subtitle:** "Collaborate, engage and stay connected with your mentors and peers"

**Mission Statement (from website):**
> "At Pyoopil, we are committed to creating technology solutions in the field of education for students, educators and educational organizations. We aspire to build an organization driven by cutting-edge technology and a mission to enable millions to teach and learn seamlessly."

**Core Features:**
1. **Course Creation Tool** — Any individual could create a course with a sequence of concepts
2. **Learning Path Builder** — Sequence of concepts; for each concept, create own content or curate from existing resources
3. **Chat Channels** — Per-concept chat channels for peer collaboration
4. **Mentor Channels** — Dedicated 1-on-1 channels with mentors
5. **Announcements** — Mentors could push important updates
6. **Mobile-first** — Android app on Google Play (`com.pyoopil.pyoopil2`)

### Product Evolution

1. **Phase 1:** Peer-to-peer collaboration learning network for campuses
2. **Multiple failures and pivots** (per HMT's own account in BuzzInContent/MediaNews4U)
3. **Phase 2 (final):** B2B Corporate Learning SaaS
   - Marketplace of corporate trainers
   - SaaS platform for L&D divisions to run training programs end-to-end
   - Mobile-based SaaS for employees to consume training on the go
   - Elements of peer engagement and mentorship built in

### Technical Description (from various sources)
- "Mobile-based SaaS (Software-as-a-service) product used by corporates to deploy online training programmes"
- "Learning product with elements of peer engagement and mentorship built in"
- Web-based school and learning management system (Tracxn classification)

## The Acquisition

### Date & Type
- **Date:** October 17, 2016
- **Type:** Acqui-hire (buyout primarily for skills/expertise of staff, not products/services)
- **Amount:** Undisclosed
- **Acquirer:** UpGrad (U Education Management Pvt. Ltd.), co-founded by Ronnie Screwvala

### Strategic Rationale

**For UpGrad:**
- Entry into the **$1 billion+ corporate training market** in India
- Goal to build a **$100 million revenue B2B business** in 5 years
- B2B learning needs differ from B2C; couldn't use existing product
- Got access to **product features** that would have taken longer to build in-house
- In discussions with 30+ companies for customised programmes
- Would offer customised programmes in data analytics, product management, digital marketing
- Also targeting SME segment and pre-joining training for fresh talent

**For Pyoopil:**
- Shared drive and values on approaching online education
- Scale and resources of UpGrad platform

### Post-Acquisition
- Pyoopil team joined UpGrad
- Continued building learning products for working professionals
- UpGrad partnered with Cognizant, Genpact, Gramener, Star TV, Disney, Uber, Google, Microsoft, BookMyShow for content/case studies
- UpGrad expected $20M B2C topline in the next financial year
- Pyoopil was UpGrad's **first acquisition** (followed by Acadview in 2018, CohortPlus in 2019)
