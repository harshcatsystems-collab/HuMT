## 1. Vinay Kumar Singhal — CEO & Co-Founder

- **Full Name:** Vinay Kumar Singhal
- **Role:** Co-Founder & CEO, STAGE (and formerly WittyFeed/Vatsana Technologies)
- **Email:** vinay@stage.in
- **LinkedIn:** linkedin.com/in/vinaykrsinghal
- **X/Twitter:** @vinaykrsinghal
- **Crunchbase:** crunchbase.com/person/vinay-singhal-cc6b
- **The Org:** theorg.com/org/stage-2/org-chart/vinay-kumar-singhal

### Background
- **Hometown:** Nunsar, Haryana — first engineering graduate in his family
- **Education:** B.Tech Computer Science, SRM University, Chennai (2009–2013)
- **Siblings:** Brother of Parveen Singhal (co-founder)
- **Entrepreneurial journey started 2010** with "Badlega Bharat" — online youth awareness organization while at SRM
- Started a Facebook page "Amazing Things in the World" that gained 1M followers in 6 months
- Built stupidstation.com which generated substantial AdSense revenue
- **WittyFeed (2014–2019):** Co-founded with Parveen & Shashank. Grew to world's #2 viral content platform (after BuzzFeed). 120M users, Rs 200Cr valuation, Top 200 global website. Bootstrapped — Rs 25.9 Cr revenue in FY16, 15-20% net margins. Wiped out Nov 26, 2018 by Facebook's post-Cambridge Analytica purge.
- **STAGE (2019–present):** Built from WittyFeed's ashes. Now Rs 672Cr valuation, 5.5M+ paying subscribers.
- **Shark Tank India S2 (Jan 2023):** Pitched at ₹300Cr valuation, got deal from Aman Gupta, Namita Thapar, Peyush Bansal
- **Beyond The Tank S4 (Feb 2025):** Returned showing 60x revenue growth. "₹50 lakh per day."
- Has an AI EA named "Bittu"

### Key Quotes
- On HMT: "Tripathi exhibited a commitment similar to that of a cofounder" and "our own personal Paul Graham"
- "Our growth isn't accidental. Every subscriber who joins STAGE isn't just a user — they're custodians of their culture."

### Relationship to HMT
- Met HMT at SRM in 2010 — HMT was the college senior who emailed Vinay 4 days after his first website launch (May 14, 2010). From HMT, Vinay learned what a "co-founder" is. 16-year bond.
