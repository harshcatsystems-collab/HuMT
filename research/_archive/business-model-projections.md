## Strategic Takeaways

1. **Culture-as-unit economics**: Each language is treated as a separate P&L — clean way to track investment vs returns
2. **Haryanvi is the cash cow** — only profitable culture, funding expansion into others
3. **Bhojpuri is the growth engine** — projected ₹1.6B revenue by FY27, biggest single culture
4. **Renewal revenue > new subscriber revenue** from FY25 — proves product-market fit and retention
5. **Marketing-heavy model**: 65-73% of costs, classic consumer subscription playbook
6. **Path to ₹300 Cr revenue by FY27** — but losses widen to ~₹692M (₹69 Cr) — need funding runway
7. **4 more cultures ready** (Awadhi, Maithili, Magahi, Bundeli) — next wave after Bhojpuri proves out
