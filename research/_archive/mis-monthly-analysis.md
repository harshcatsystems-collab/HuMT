## 3. Key Insights

### Revenue
- **Peak revenue month:** Dec '24 at ₹15.17 Cr
- **Jan '26 revenue:** ₹13.64 Cr (recovering after mid-2025 dip)
- **FY25 total (Apr '24 – Mar '25):** ~₹134.7 Cr (aligns with Inc42's ₹135 Cr claim, NOT ₹111 Cr)
- **FY24 total (Apr '23 – Mar '24):** ~₹28.4 Cr
- **Revenue is 99%+ subscription** — Brand and Distribution are negligible

### Profitability
- **Sep '24 was the FIRST operationally profitable month** (+1.7% op margin)
- **Feb '25 was the BEST month ever** (+55.4% op margin — marketing cut to ₹1.5 Cr from ₹21 Cr)
- **Mar-Apr '25 also profitable** (~5% and 2% op margin)
- **Since May '25: back to losses** — marketing spend ramped back up for Bhojpuri/Gujarati expansion
- **Jan '26: -56.6% op margin** — heavy content + marketing spend

### Subscribers
- **Peak active subs: 2,461,851 (Jan '25)** — NOT 7.5M as press claims
- **Current (Jan '26): 1,818,018 active subscribers** — down 26% from peak
- **The 7.5M claim is likely LIFETIME subscribers** (cumulative ever-subscribed), not active
- **New subscriber acquisition collapsed Feb-Apr '25** (69K→33K→28K/month) when marketing was cut
- **Since Sep '25: recovering** — back to 200K+ new subs/month
- **Renewals > New subs since Aug '24** — business shifted from acquisition to renewal-driven

### CAC
- **CAC spiked to ₹2,108 in Apr '25** (lowest acquisition month)
- **Now stabilized at ₹450-520** — back to healthy range
- **Best CAC: ₹217 (Feb '25)** — but that was with minimal acquisition spend

### Cash Position
- **Jan '26 cash: ₹31.92 Cr** (₹3,192 Lakhs)
- **Runway: 4 months** at current burn
- **Peak cash: ₹92.59 Cr (Dec '24)** — post Series B infusion
- **Burning through cash quickly** — from ₹93 Cr to ₹32 Cr in 13 months

### Gross Margin
- **Declining trend:** From 95%+ (mid-2024) to 67.6% (Jan '26)
- **Cause:** Content costs spiked — ₹4.06 Cr content cost in Jan '26 (vs ₹0.37 Cr in Apr '24)
- **Dubbed content costs rising** with Bhojpuri/Gujarati expansion

### Marketing Spend
- **Peak: ₹21 Cr in Dec '24** (massive acquisition push)
- **Then slashed to ₹1.5 Cr in Feb '25** (caused new sub collapse)
- **Now: ₹13.2 Cr in Jan '26** — moderate spend
- **Performance marketing: ₹10.4 Cr** of total ₹13.2 Cr (79%)
- **Brand marketing: effectively ₹0** since Nov '25

### ⚠️ Critical Data Discrepancy
- **Press/investor claims: 7.5M subscribers, 5.5M+ paying**
- **MIS actual: 1.82M active subscribers (Jan '26)**
- **Likely explanation:** "7.5M households" = lifetime cumulative subscribers (all who ever subscribed). Active at any given time is ~1.8M. The 72% annual churn means most of the 7.5M lifetime subs have churned out.
