## 7. Summary: How HMT Communicates

1. **Email = intake channel, not conversation channel.** Receives 60–80/week, sends ~2/week. Work happens in Slack/meetings/WhatsApp.
2. **Routing-layer CEO pattern.** Receives → delegates to right person → exits thread. Ultra-efficient.
3. **Calendar is the real operating system.** ~18 meetings/week, heavily mid-week (Wed/Thu). Aggressively declines to manage load.
4. **Terse, action-oriented.** 1–4 sentences max. Signs as "HMT." No ceremony.
5. **Fast on high-priority, slow on low-priority.** Samsung delegation same-day (Sunday). Investor emails can sit unread for months.
6. **Inner circle:** Vinay, Parveen (co-founders), Pranay, Shashank, Nikhil — these people get meeting time and email responses.
7. **Weekend boundary respected** on calendar (zero meetings) but will reply to urgent email on Sundays.
8. **35–40% of inbox is noise.** Bank alerts, invoices, newsletters. No star system used. No filters visible.
9. **Post-sprint/board recovery pattern:** Aggressively declines meetings after intense periods.
10. **Evening availability for external:** US timezone calls happen 20:30–22:00 IST (Anthropic, Goodwater, NVIDIA).
