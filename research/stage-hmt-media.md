## Executive Summary

HMT's media footprint around STAGE is concentrated in **three key moments**: (1) his 2018 appointment as CPSO at Vatsana/WittyFeed, (2) his Jan 2024 elevation to Co-founder, and (3) the Mar 2025 Series B announcement where he's quoted as Co-founder & CPO. He is notably **absent** from Shark Tank India S2 coverage (Jan 2023) and the Beyond The Tank S4 segment (Feb 2025), which only feature Vinay, Parveen, and Shashank. No standalone interviews, podcasts, or conference talks by HMT were found.

## HMT Biographical Facts (compiled from all sources)

- **Education:** B.E./B.Tech in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from SRM University (now SRMIST), Chennai
- **Young India Fellowship** by IFRE and University of Pennsylvania
- **Entrepreneurial timeline:**
  - **2010:** Co-founded Vilikh Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (robotics, embedded systems, e-commerce) — operated from SRM incubation centre, ₹12 lakh turnover in early year
  - **2013:** Founded Pyoopil Education Technologies (P2P learning → pivoted to B2B corporate learning SaaS)
  - **Oct 2016:** Pyoopil acqui-hired by UpGrad; HMT spent ~2 years in leadership at UpGrad
  - **2017:** Angel investor in WittyFeed/Vatsana Technologies and TapChief
  - **~2019-2021:** Venture Partner at First Cheque LLP (micro VC)
  - **Oct 2018:** Joined Vatsana as Chief Product & Strategy Officer
  - **2019:** Involved in conceiving and building STAGE from ground up
  - **Jan 2024:** Officially elevated to Co-Founder of STAGE
- **Key relationship with Vinay Singhal:** Goes back to 2010 ("college senior" per Vinay's tweet) — ~14 years of association before co-founder title. HMT was an investor in Vatsana before joining.
- **Current title variations:** "Co-Founder" (most sources), "Chief Product Officer & Co-Founder" (ET/ANI Series B), "Chief Product & Strategy Officer & Co-Founder" (ANI)
