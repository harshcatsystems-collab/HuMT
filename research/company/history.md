### Product Evolution

**Phase 1: Campus Peer Learning (2013–2015)**
- Tagline: "Communities around courses — Collaborate, engage and stay connected with your mentors and peers"
- Features: Course creation tool, chat channels per concept, mentor channels, announcements, classroom model, engagement analytics, Android app
- Tech: CakePHP 2.x backend, AngularJS ~1.2.20 frontend, Grunt build, Bower, Travis CI, Apache hosting

**Phase 2: B2B Corporate Training SaaS (2015–2016)**
- After "multiple failures and pivots" (HMT's own words)
- Marketplace of corporate trainers + SaaS platform for L&D divisions
- Mobile-based SaaS for employees to consume training on the go

### The Acquisition

- **Date:** October 17, 2016
- **Type:** Acqui-hire | **Amount:** Undisclosed
- **Acquirer:** UpGrad (U Education Management Pvt. Ltd.) — founded March 2015 by Ronnie Screwvala, Mayank Kumar, Ravijot Chugh, Prabhav Phalgun. Initial investment: ₹100 crore (~$16M) from Screwvala.
- **Strategic rationale:** Entry into $1B+ Indian corporate training market. Goal: $100M B2B revenue in 5 years. Needed B2B product features UpGrad couldn't build fast enough. In discussions with 30+ companies.
- **UpGrad ecosystem at acquisition:** Clients — Mahindra, HCL, Tata, Cognizant, Genpact. Partners — Google, Disney India, Star TV, Uber, Microsoft, BookMyShow, Gramener, Ola, Paytm, Snapdeal, IIIT-B, Accel Partners.
- **Post-acquisition:** Pyoopil team joined UpGrad. Was their first acquisition — followed by Acadview (2018), CohortPlus (2019). No redirect from pyoopil.com; domain abandoned and hijacked by 2019.
