## 5. HR Infrastructure

### Aon Talent Strategy Engagement (Feb 2025, ₹26.5L)
5 modules for STAGE at ~80 employees:

1. **Vision, Mission & Values** (3 weeks, ₹8L) — Leadership workshops, employee surveys, Forbes-style drafting, CEO/Board validation
2. **Level & Title Architecture** (4-5 weeks, ₹10L) — Job evaluation for 80-100 roles against Aon's 5 compensable factors; unified level structure
3. **Technical Competency Framework** (4-6 weeks, ₹10L) — P1 (Learner) through P4 (Expert) proficiency definitions
4. **Behavioral Competency Framework** (4 weeks, ₹3L) — Growth Vectors:
   - Look Beyond The Obvious (Foresight, Big picture, Detail orientation)
   - Outcomes Matter More Than The Craft (Outcome orientation, Solution mindset)
   - It's Only Impossible Until It's Done (Ambition, Experimenting, Resilience)
   - Responsibility Is Taken Not Given (Learning orientation, Ownership, Empowerment)
   - Success Is A Team Sport (Collaboration, Collective Accountability)
5. **Compensation Benchmarking** (3 weeks, ₹5L) — Peer basket, compa-ratio, market positioning

Total ₹36L → discounted to ₹26.5L (26%). Timeline: 8-11 weeks. Payment: 50% signing, 50% delivery.
