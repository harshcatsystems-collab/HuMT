## Key Quotes & Insights

### On Identity
- "A language is not just about access, it's about identity."
- "We are carrying an inherent identity of another... it's the identity that speaks to your core, to your emotional core, and that is generally associated to your native tongue."
- "Language is about identity and not class." (closing line)

### On STAGE's Business
- 12 million users in two dialects (Haryanvi & Rajasthani)
- 1.2 million paying users (quarterly subscription)
- Already profitable in Haryanvi, soon profitable in Rajasthani
- 19,000+ dialects in India; STAGE has ~20 more in sight

### On AI
- Built an AI bot that converses in Haryanvi (a language with NO script and NO literature)
- Bot detects user tonality and adapts — can switch between Haryanvi and Hindi
- Paradigm shift: from reactive software to proactive AI — the bot realized a user was struggling and proactively reached out
- Hiring Haryanvi/Rajasthani-speaking customer support team reduced refund rates by 50%

### On Perception
- "There is a perception that if someone speaks in their native dialect, there's a presumption of the socioeconomic strip that they come from. Let me also tell you that our users mail us in impeccable English."
- STAGE users range from "an Olympian to a startup CEO to an IT professional to a government employee to a farmer and even a taxi driver"
