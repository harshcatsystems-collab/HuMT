## How to Use This

**One question → one file:**
- Money question → `financials/model.md`
- Who is someone → `../memory/people.md` (quick) or `people/research.md` (deep)
- HMT's media presence → `media/appearances.md`
- How STAGE operates internally → `company/operations.md`
- Market/competitors → `company/market.md`
- HMT's pre-STAGE career → `company/history.md`
- Communication patterns → `comms/patterns.md`
- Anything about STAGE as a whole → `STAGE-MASTER-BRIEF.md`
- Anything about HMT as a person → `../USER.md`
