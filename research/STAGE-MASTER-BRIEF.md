### Founding Story

After Facebook's post-Cambridge Analytica purge wiped out **WittyFeed** (world's #2 content platform, 120M users, ₹200Cr valuation) overnight on **November 26, 2018**, the four founders — Vinay Singhal, Parveen Singhal, Shashank Vaishnav, and Harsh Mani Tripathi — rebuilt from the ashes.

54 of 90 WittyFeed employees stayed at **25% salary + equity**. After 4-5 months of brainstorming, they identified a gap: India's 500M+ dialect-speaking population had zero premium content in their own languages. STAGE launched **November 1, 2019** with a seed round of ₹1.5 Cr from Venture Catalysts.

### Mission

Validate, reinforce, and celebrate socio-cultural identities through premium regional content. "Language is about identity, not class."

### The Lesson Burned Into Their DNA

**Never build on platforms you don't control. Own your distribution.**

## 3. Product

### What STAGE Is
A **subscription-only** (no ads) OTT platform for India's **dialect-level** regional content — not Hindi or broad regional languages, but specific dialects: **Haryanvi, Rajasthani, Bhojpuri**. Content includes web series, films, short films, comedy, poetry, folk performances, and "STAGE Shorts" (vertical micro-dramas).

### How It Works
- **100% subscription model** — ₹1 seven-day trial → **₹199/month** subscription via **UPI autopay** (major revenue unlocker mid-2023). Previously ₹399/3 months.
- **Affordable "household" plans** targeting rural/semi-urban families
- **CAC dropped from ₹6,000 to ₹250 per user** through product-market fit and community-driven growth
- **Active trial experiments:** 1-day trial winning over 7-day — 6.4% higher net new subscribers per 100 paywall views (8.83 vs 8.30, per Feb 2026 Slack data)
- **Payment stack:** UPI autopay via PhonePe, Paytm, PayU, Yesbizz, Razorpay, Setu. ⚠️ Paytm trial rate dropped from 3% to 1.56% (Feb 2026 — "critical business impact on revenue")
### Content Strategy
- **Commission originals** with local artists, local stories, local languages
- STAGE must **build entire content ecosystems from scratch** — Haryanvi/Rajasthani/Bhojpuri lack organized film industries
- Haryana now has **150 production houses** working with STAGE regularly
- **50 original movies/year** in each market
- **700-800 hours** of original content (as of early 2025)
- **70-80 titles in one-year pipeline** (per Feb 16, 2026 All Hands)
- **~4 new titles/month** (per May 2025 product meeting)
- **Content cost:** ₹4-5L/movie initially → ₹25L now (vs ₹50L-1Cr regional OTT, exponentially more for Netflix)
- **External pitches:** Rajkapoor Studio pitched "LAW MASTER" Haryanvi movie (₹4.5Cr) + "VIBE ON Season 2" musical reality show
- Hit titles: *Videshi Bahu*, *Kaand 2010*, *Bhawani*, *Dada Lakhmi* (National Award-winning)
- **Active productions (Feb 2026):** *Videshi Bahu 2* (HR), *Chidibaaz* (HR, film screening Feb 15 in Rohtak), *Swaang* (HR, microdrama shooting), *Chuppa Raaz* (shoot started Feb 12), *Jaan Legi Sonam* (BH, web series), *Maha Punarjanam* (multi), *Saanwari* (multi), *Anda Gang*, *Muawja/Kayantar*, YRF Rajasthani microdrama (released Feb 14)
- **Content compliance:** Anushka Shrivastava manages; suicide/substance use disclaimers required for micro dramas. Review channel: #content-compliance-hiren
- **Title Intelligence Platform:** Priyanka built piracy link detection tool via BMAD
- Pre-STAGE community assets: **"InnerVoice,"** **"Unheard,"** and **"Bhasad"** — built at WittyFeed, became India's largest communities for poets, comedians, storytellers. These seeded the STAGE content ecosystem.
