## Part I: The Strategic Framework (Slides 3-7)

### The Three Pillars of Growth

| Pillar | Function | Key Metrics |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **Acquisition & (Re)Activation** | New users discover STAGE, install, sign up, start trial | IR, TR, D0TCR, SR, Sub CAC |
| **Retention & Re-Engagement** | Trial converts to paid. Subscribers stay, watch, renew. | M0, Watch Retention, SCR, Renewal |
| **Churned Re-activation** | Churned users reactivated. Dormant re-engaged. | Re-acquisition, Dormant recovery |

> "Without acquisition — there is nobody to retain. Without retention — constant churn. Without re-acquisition — leaking bucket that never fills."

### The Mindset Shift: CAC-First → LTV-First

**Before (CAC-First Thinking):**
- Optimise for lowest CAC possible
- Success = cheap installs
- Content, Promo & Performance work in silos
- No visibility beyond acquisition
- Churn is "someone else's problem"
- **Result:** "Cheap users who don't stay"

**After (LTV as North Star):**

| Function | Metrics Feeding LTV |
|----------|---------------------|
| Content | WTR · Completion · Depth |
| Promo | Hook · Hold · CTR |
| Performance | IR · TR · D0TCR · SR · Sub CAC |
| Retention | M0 · Watch Retention · SCR · Renewal |

**North Star Goal:** LTV positive by Month 3

### Full Funnel Metrics Cascade

```

├── Overall TAM
├── Watch Through Rate (WTR)
├── Completion Rate
└── Depth

├── Hook Rate (thumb stop)

```

### The 5 Promo Shifts

1. **Platform-Selling, Not Title-Selling**
   - Make users feel the platform has infinite depth — not just one show worth watching

2. **Make Long-Form the Default**
   - 7–15 min promos across all cultures
   - Short-form becomes the exception

3. **Own the Gujarati Scale-Up**
   - Budget is 2×-ing
   - Creative output must match — or spend advantage is wasted

4. **Find Promos That Bring Users Who Stay**
   - Not just users who are curious

5. **Never Be the Bottleneck**
   - Build a pipeline deep enough so no other pod waits on promo
   - Document every decision: Format · length · angle · platform · result
   - "This manual phase is the dataset for the future agentic system"

### Key Insights

**Promo Type Performance:**
- Single title / content-first promos ❌
- Bouquet Value & Platform promos ✅
- "Platform value promos outperform single title promos consistently"

**CSL (Content-Specific Landing) Results:**
- CSL improved IR → better overall efficiency
- CAC ↓ ₹44
- TCR +2%

## Part V: Product & AI (Slides 20-27)

### The D0-TCR Problem

**Core insight:** 50% of trial takers cancel within 20 mins, heavily skewed towards the first 10 mins.

**The Analogy:**
> "Our Play Store is like a clothing shop. A user sees an ad of the variety we offer — suits, sarees, shirts. They visit the shop. If they don't find a mannequin wearing what was promised..."

**Root Cause Analysis:**
- High IR but low TR = users come for the content, not the subscription
- Standard paywall shown cold creates friction before emotional investment
- Low paying propensity at install

### Product Strategy: Engagement-Depth First

**Principle:** Create value for the user BEFORE the paywall — before they reach a cold paywall with zero emotional investment.

### Concepts Explored

| Concept | Status |
|---------|--------|
| SA-with-direct < SA-with-paywall | Discarded |
| SA at 1st paywall view | Tested |
| SA at 2nd paywall view | 2nd view is winner |

### Special Access Strategy

**Plan:** Special Access at First Paywall
- Fix: increase paying propensity through Special Access before the paywall ask

**Tested approaches:**
1. First 10–15 min free, no paywall → "You're into the story — watch the rest for ₹1"
2. Episode 1 plays in full (verify Ep 1 ends on cliffhanger — flag to content if not)
3. First 3–4 episodes free. Gate = soft overlay while next ep buffers (not a hard stop)
4. "[Character] just made a choice that changes everything" (narrative hook)

### What Was Built in 10 Days

(AI/Product system for performance marketing automation)
- Full audit trail on every decision
- Expand coverage as confidence builds

### Lever Framework

| Lever | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| **Lever A** | Reduce drop-off before any investment is built |
| **Lever B — Exploration UI** | Build a second content hook before the window closes |
| **Lever C — Forward Pull UI** | Non-content engagement — tambola, games |

### Non-content Engagement Ideas

- Trial tambola card on consumption page (D7 deferred reward)
- Watch streak — Day 1 declared
